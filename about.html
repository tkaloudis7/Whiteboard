About Whiteboard

Whiteboard is a task management application that helps students organize their studies and meet their deadlines.



Key Features

  • Task Manager: Add your daily to-do list and its priority level
  • Deadline Tracker: Stay on track with your deadlines
  • Simple & Fast: No sign ups and logins. We want to make your life easier.
